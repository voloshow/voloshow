## About Voloshow

**Voloshow** is an AI image and video creative studio built for creators, marketers, ecommerce teams, and product builders who need more than a single prompt-to-image result.

Start with an AI image, continue with editing tools, prepare product photos, turn still images into video, and keep finished assets connected to public works, prompts, and remixable inspiration.

## What You Can Do

- **AI Image Generation** - Create original images from text prompts and visual ideas.
- **Image-to-Image Workflows** - Use reference images to guide edits, variations, and transformations.
- **Image Tools** - Remove backgrounds, prepare clean product visuals, resize assets, convert files, and continue editing after generation.
- **Product Photo Creation** - Build ecommerce-ready product visuals for listings, ads, and social campaigns.
- **Image to Video** - Animate still images with motion prompts and video-oriented creative flows.
- **Prompt & Gallery Discovery** - Explore public AI works, prompts, tags, and remixable examples.

That means creators can move from idea to finished visual assets without jumping across unrelated tools.
